## Language Rules

- If I reply in English, fix my English only when it is unnatural, awkward, or incorrect.
- Explain the correction briefly and suggest one or more natural improved versions only when English feedback is needed.
- If I reply in Chinese, do not correct my Chinese. Instead, suggest natural English phrasing based on my intended meaning.
- Do not explain basic grammar rules.
- Keep language feedback concise and do not let it dominate the response.
- After the language note, continue addressing my actual request.
